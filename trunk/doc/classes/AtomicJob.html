splitJob()

Splits a job in two parts for coallocation. The nodes are divided by two, and all other parameters are left intact. So one job is split in two concurrent jobs. The job IDs are the one of the original job. We expect that they are embedded in a MultiJob. Returns an array containing the two cloned jobs.
